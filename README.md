A practical web-based calculator to analyze your salary breakdown and visualize monthly expenses.

## Features

- Calculate net monthly savings from annual CTC
- Tax deduction calculation
- Monthly expense tracking (Rent, EMI, Expenses)
- Interactive pie chart visualization
- Clean, responsive design

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Enter your details:
   - CTC (Annual salary)
   - Tax percentage
   - Monthly rent
   - Monthly EMI
   - Monthly expenses
3. Click "Calculate" to view breakdown and chart

- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Chart.js
